Assignment 1: Color Cubes

Below are the controls for this assignment:

  • 'IJKL' move the camera forward, back, and side-to-side
  • 'UP' and 'DOWN' arrow keys move camera up and down
  • 'LEFT' and 'RIGHT' arrow keys rotate camera side to side
  • 'w' widens field of view. 'n' narrows it.
  • 'r' undos any movement and fov changes you have made.
  • '+' toggles a crosshair that appears on the screen.
  • 'c' cycles the colors of the cubes.
