Final Project: Piano Hero

This was my final group project for CS 174A, and I worked with my fellow classmates Robert Posada and John Stucky.

It is a piano simulator which has two game modes: free play and Piano Hero mode. You can either freely play the piano, or play a game mode similar to "Guitar Hero", where you try to press the piano keys as the note indicators fall down.
