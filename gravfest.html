A game I created at SB Hacks 2017.

It is a platformer game where the player can control the direction of gravity, and use that ability to navigate
through the various levels. All textures and assets were pulled off of the open source website opengameart.org

Controls are as follows:

WASD: Directional Movement
IJKL: Change direction of gravity
Space: Jump
E: Use item (door, switch)
ESC: Pause Menu

The goal of the game is to reach the door at the end of each level. Have fun!
